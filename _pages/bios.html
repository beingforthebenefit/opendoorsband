Nate Fink

Bassist

Originally from the DC area, Nate has been living on the west coast most of his adult life. Growing up, he got into 60s and 70s rock, listening to Zeppelin, The Beatles, and of course The Doors on repeat. Since moving to the bay area in 2014, Nate has continued playing music. He served as the singer and bassist for All Day Breakfast. Most recently Nate has been handling bass, backing vocals, and sometimes rhythm guitar for Greg Hoy and The Boys who recently completed tours on both coasts. Now he lays down a solid foundation of killer bass to the OPEN DOORS musical assault on your senses.
